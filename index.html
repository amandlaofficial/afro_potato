AFRO POTATE

Before any tuber or grain is unloaded at the facility, samples are taken to ensure it passes inspection. Our products are tested for moisture content, infestation, unsound kernels/seeds, foreign material and Aflatoxin. Once they have passed inspection they are unloaded and stored into large bins/ silos or in the go down. Storing farm produce is a science. The right moisture, temperatures and air must be maintained or the produce may mould, sprout, or ferment. During storage the irish potatoes and grain may go through a fumigation process to eliminate insect pests. Our products are stored according to colour and other quality considerations.
