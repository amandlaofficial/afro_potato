ABOUT AFRO POTATO

Quality African Potatoes

Afro Potato started its operations in 1999 with a modern maize milling factory in Kabale. It processes high quality flour, potatos, kalo and millet porridge, using the best processes and technologies available.

Afro Potato has since diversified its operations to milling of maize, porridge and millet flour.

Our Products

Afro Potato, African Kalo & Afro Maize Flour! These are the flagship brand of the company and key contenders and leaders in their respective categories.

Afro Potato products are readily available to consumers in the general & modern trade and has an integrated distribution system that ensure customers have access to the range of products at their convenience..
